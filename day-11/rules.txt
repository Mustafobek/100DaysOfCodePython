Rules of BlackJack or 21


----------------------------------------------
Values

2 = 2
3 = 3
...
10 = 10

J, Q, K = 10

Scores

if total score of player cards is more than 21 then player lose
else if total scores of players are equal then draw
else if total scores are less then 21, then winner is that whose total score is higher 
if Dealer ends up smaller than 17, then it must take another card
